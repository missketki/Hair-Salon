About Ketki Hair Salon

A hair salon is a business that offers hair styling and other cosmetic services. Usually, a hair salon will have a team of hair stylists to provide these services. The hair stylists are required to be licensed cosmetologists trained as either hair stylists, general cosmetologists, estheticians, barbers, or manicurists.
